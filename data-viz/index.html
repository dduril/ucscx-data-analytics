Data visualization principles and techniques using Tableau 10.2 and publicly available datasets for training and development. Additional topics include asset management, data preparation and manipulation, joining multiple datasets, version control, documentation, best practices and visualization principles.

Course focus on examples from the Tableau Online tutorials to learn effective ways of developing data visualizations and dashboards incorporating tables, charts and maps.
