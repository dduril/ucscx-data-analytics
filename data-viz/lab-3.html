Histogram Chart

  • Create a Bin and assign the Quantity to the appropriate buckets.
  • Select Sales mesaure and use the Create Bin option. (This goes to Column).
  • Tableau suggests a bin size automatically. Adjust this number to your preference.
  • Select Quantity. (This goes to Rows).
